\chapter*{Vespers Insert for Saturday, 6 Nov. 2021}

 \textit{Vespers today is based on the following Sunday, which is the Third of November and the 24th after Pentecost. All is said as in the main booklet until the Magnificat.}

\section*{Collect}

\begin{latinenglishsection}

\latinenglish{
	\Vbar.~Dómine exáudi oratiónem meam.\\
	\Rbar.~Et clamor meus ad te véniat.
	
	Orémus.
	Famíliam tuam, qu\'{\ae}sumus, Dómine, contínua pietáte custódi: ut, quæ in sola spe grátiæ c{\oe}léstis innítitur, tua semper protectióne muniátur.
	Per Dóminum.
	\Rbar.~Amen.
}{
	Lord, hear my prayer.
	\Rbar.~And let my cry come unto Thee.
	
	Let us pray.
	O Lord, we beseech Thee to keep Thy household continually under Thy mercy: that as it leans only upon the hope of Thy heavenly grace, so it may always
	be protected by Thy mighty power.
	Through Our Lord.
	\Rbar.~Amen.
}

\end{latinenglishsection}

\section*{Commemoration of the Octave of All Saints}

\textit{\textnormal{Ant.} Oh! how glorious is the kingdom where all the Saints rejoice with Christ; clothed in white robes, they follow the Lamb whithersoever he goeth!}

\begin{rubricbox}

After the cantor intones up to the asterisk, all \textbf{sit} and join in singing.

\end{rubricbox}

\gresetinitiallines{1}
\gregorioscore{comm-oct-all_saints}

\begin{rubricbox}

All \textbf{stand} for the prayer.

\end{rubricbox}

\begin{latinenglishsection}

\latinenglish{
	\Vbar.~Exsultábunt Sancti in glória.\\
	\Rbar.~Lætabúntur in cubílibus suis.\\
	
	Orémus.
	Omnípotens sempitérne Deus, qui nos ómnium Sanctórum tuórum mérita sub una tribuísti celebritáte venerári: qu\'{\ae}sumus; ut desiderátam nobis tuæ
	propitiatiónis abundántiam, multiplicátis intercessóribus, largiáris.
	Per Dóminum.
}{
	The just exult in their glory.
	\Rbar.~They shout for joy on their beds.
	
	Let us pray.
	Almighty, eternal God, Who hast granted us to honor the merits of all Thy Saints in a single solemn festival, bestow on us, we beseech Thee, through their
	manifold intercession, that abundance of Thy mercy for which we yearn.
	Through Our Lord.
}

\end{latinenglishsection}
